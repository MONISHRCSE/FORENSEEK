# FORENSEEK
# AI-Powered Forensic Triage & Investigative Intelligence Platform

---

# 1. Project Overview

FORENSEEK is an AI-powered forensic triage and investigative intelligence platform developed to assist investigative agencies, forensic departments, and law enforcement teams in processing and correlating large volumes of forensic and digital evidence efficiently.

Modern criminal investigations often involve multiple fragmented evidence sources such as:
- CCTV footage
- witness statements
- autopsy reports
- geolocation records
- mobile metadata
- audio recordings
- forensic observations

Investigators typically analyze these sources manually, which is:
- time-consuming
- difficult to scale
- prone to human oversight
- inefficient during time-sensitive investigations

FORENSEEK addresses this challenge by acting as an AI investigative assistant that automatically:
- processes multimodal evidence
- extracts important entities
- builds chronological timelines
- detects inconsistencies
- correlates forensic and digital evidence
- generates explainable investigative insights

The platform combines:
- Artificial Intelligence
- Computer Vision
- Natural Language Processing
- Audio Intelligence
- Evidence Correlation
- Explainable AI
- Visual Investigation Analytics

to support investigators during the critical early stages of criminal investigations.

Unlike traditional forensic tools that focus on a single evidence source, FORENSEEK creates a unified intelligence layer that connects fragmented information into structured investigative reasoning.

The system is designed strictly as an investigative assistance platform and NOT as a replacement for forensic experts, medical professionals, or legal authorities.

Its primary goal is to:
- reduce investigation delays
- improve evidence organization
- detect hidden contradictions
- prioritize investigative leads
- support faster and more informed decision-making

Manual analysis is:

FORENSEEK aims to solve this challenge by providing AI-assisted evidence analysis and explainable investigative intelligence.

# 5.7 Contradiction Detection Engine

The contradiction detection engine is the core innovation of FORENSEEK.

The AI compares:
- witness statements
- CCTV timestamps
- GPS metadata
- forensic timelines
- audio transcripts
- digital records

and automatically identifies inconsistencies.

## Example
Witness Statement:
"I left the location at 9 PM."

CCTV Evidence:
Suspect detected at 10:12 PM.

Witness statement conflicts with CCTV evidence.

The system highlights:
- conflicting timelines
- suspicious overlaps
- impossible movement patterns
- inconsistent witness testimony

This feature helps investigators quickly identify suspicious evidence and prioritize further investigation.
